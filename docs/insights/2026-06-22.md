**Proposed new marts/metrics**

1. **Stop‑Delay Heat‑Map Mart** – A dimension table keyed by `stop_id` with pre‑aggregated metrics: median delay, OTP%, total observations, and a “cluster tag” (e.g., BRIDGE_ST, MCLELLAN_HWY). Refresh daily; enable quick drill‑down from a GIS layer to see where delays exceed a configurable threshold (e.g., +5 min).  

2. **Route‑Stop Lag Ratio Metric** – A fact view that joins the stop‑delay mart to route itineraries and computes `lag_ratio = median_delay / scheduled_headway`. Values > 1 flag routes where the stop delay is larger than the intended spacing, highlighting where schedule compression is feasible (especially for the early stops).  

3. **Early‑Schedule Cushion Index** – A KPI table that aggregates, per route and per stop, the average early arrival (negative median) and the corresponding passenger‑load factor (if available). This index quantifies “over‑scheduled” capacity, supporting targeted schedule reductions on routes 8, the early stops, and the Walpole shuttle.
